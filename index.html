About Me

Hello! My name is Michał. I'm an aspiring web developer from Poland with a burning passion for digital art and graphic design. Blogs, portfolios, online shops - name it, I bring you exactly that. With technical support too!

I'm native in Polish, fluent in English (CAE certificate on C1 level) and conversational in German. Once tried learning French too, but programming took higher priority. Tant pis!

I am an artist first and foremost. Web design is an artform to me just as much as digital art is, but that doesn't mean I value form over function, because actually, I put an emphasis on both. My projects are both effective and attractive.

Want to see for yourself? Try out my services and I guarantee you won't be disappointed.
